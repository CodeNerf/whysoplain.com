My Story

Paris Plain first fell in love with the art of programming when he was young. Growing up, he was more fascinated with how video games were made, than actually playing them. Not long after he graduated high school he decided to take his first foray into the world of programming and hasn’t looked back since.

By the time he reached college, he had already programmed his first application. While he was studying for his Bachelor’s in IT and Master’s in Business Education, he gained a lot of experience by completing freelance work for clients from all over the world.
